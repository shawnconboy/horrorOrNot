Worth The Watch?

Below, you'll find my ratings and thoughts without spoilers of the movies listed. I personally take the time to watch all of these movies and the biggest thing I hate is when you do so and the movie ends up not being so great.

With that being said, take the time to scroll through the reviews and take a peak at the trailer if you seem interested.

One Missed Call (2008)

Rating: 7/10

One Missed Call is a movie that's based on a paranormal phenomena where characters recieve a call and a short clip audio clip of how their lives end. This movie has a decent plot. For the most part, you get the "why" for the reason these things are happening by the end of the movie.
